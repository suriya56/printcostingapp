# PrintEst Pro - User Manual

Welcome to the PrintEst Pro User Manual. This guide will help you navigate and use the application to create accurate printing quotations quickly.

## Getting Started

When you open the application, you will be prompted to log in.
- **Administrators**: Have full access to change system pricing, papers, and brackets.
- **Employees**: Can create quotations and view the history, but cannot alter base system prices.

## The Workspaces

Once logged in, you will see a navigation bar at the top with different tabs.

### 1. Single Page Estimator (Mode A)
Use this mode for simple, single-sheet jobs like **Flyers, Posters, Letterheads, or Business Cards**.
- **Job Details**: Enter the Customer Name, Job Name, and Quantity.
- **Paper Configuration**: Select the paper type from the dropdown, or choose "Custom Paper" to enter a manual price. Enter the paper size (e.g., A4, A5).
- **Post-Press**: Add lamination, binding, or other miscellaneous costs.
- **Live Breakdown**: As you type, the right-hand panel instantly updates to show the total cost, price per piece, and the exact mathematical breakdown of sheets and plates used.
- **Action**: Click "Calculate and Save" to store the quotation.

### 2. Booklet Catalog Estimator (Mode B)
Use this mode for multi-page jobs like **Books, Magazines, Catalogs, or Brochures**.
- **Inner Pages vs. Wrapper**: Books consist of inner pages and a cover (wrapper). You configure these separately.
- **Multi-Section Support**: If your book has different types of inner pages (e.g., 10 pages of glossy color, 50 pages of plain black-and-white), you can enable "Use Multiple Inner Sections" to calculate them together accurately.
- **Live Breakdown**: Like Mode A, the total price updates instantly.

### 3. Volume Scaler Simulator (Compare)
If a client asks, *"How much for 1,000 flyers vs 5,000 vs 10,000?"*, use this tab.
- Enter the basic job parameters and multiple target quantities.
- The system will calculate and display a side-by-side comparison of the total price and the per-piece cost for each quantity, helping you upsell larger print runs.

### 4. Estimations Ledger (History)
This is your archive of all saved quotations.
- **View & Download**: You can review past quotes, download them as PDF invoices, or export the data to CSV for Excel.
- **Real-Time**: If a colleague saves a new quote, it will appear here instantly.

### 5. Pricing Dashboard (Admin Only)
This is the engine room of the application.
- **Manage Costs**: Here you can update paper prices, lamination rates, plate costs, and printing brackets.
- **Instant Updates**: Any price you change and save here is **instantly updated for all employees**. If an employee is currently typing a quote, their live total will automatically recalculate using your new prices without them needing to refresh the page.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: I updated a paper price. Do my employees need to restart the app?**
A: No! The application uses real-time cloud synchronization. As soon as you hit save, their screens update instantly.

**Q: The Live Breakdown disappeared while I was typing.**
A: This issue has been fixed in the latest update. The breakdown will now safely display `₹0` or partial calculations until you finish selecting your paper profiles.

**Q: How do I export a quote to send to a customer?**
A: Go to the "Estimations Ledger" (History tab), find the quotation, and click the "PDF" button. You can also click "Export CSV" directly from the live breakdown panel while quoting.
